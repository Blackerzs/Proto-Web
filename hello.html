Riva Zackery

I grew up in the slums of Mumbai, where access to education and technology was limited. From a young age, I was fascinated by computers, inspired by stories of innovators like Bill Gates. Without owning a computer, I practiced on a discarded keyboard and earned brief daily access to a cyber café by working there.

Despite financial hardships, frequent rejections, and unstable living conditions, I taught myself to code through free online resources. Over time, I built small websites and applications, eventually gaining recognition through a competitive coding fellowship. My journey reflects resilience, self-learning, and my determination to rise above my circumstances through technology.
